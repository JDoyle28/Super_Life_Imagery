Catering

Lake Powell Groceries is proud to provide catered meals prepared by local restaurants. Prepared fresh and delivered hot to you when you want it. Catered Meals pickup request is a service that lets you choose your restaurant and meals you desire and have it delivered to your marina location. A catered meal for your first day on the boat might be just what you need! Below is a choice of 4 restaurants that will take you right to their websites where you can menu shop. All you have to do is fill out the attached request form and Lake Powell Groceries will order and pickup your desired meals.

* Premium On-Site Catering service available. Meals prepared at your location. Contact us for full details of our highly reviewed service.

How This Service Works:

  • Step 1: Download the order form.
  • Step 2: Visit the website of the restaurant of your choice by following the links below to view their menu and make your meal selections.
  • Step 3: Fill out the order form and return it to us.
    • Order by Fax: print & fill out the forms, then fax them to 1-928-832-4250.
    • Order by Email: download & edit the forms, then email them to lakepowellgroceries@gmail.com.

You will then be contacted to confirm your order and arrange for delivery.
