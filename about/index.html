Lake Powell Groceries

Provided by Captains and Crews Boat Service

Captains and Crews Boat Service and Lake Powell Groceries are owned and operated by Captain James M Doyle III, his wife Josie, and their family. Their passion has always been to provide a necessary service to the boating community while doing something they love to do. Captains and Crews Boat Service was established in 1999 with Lake Powell Groceries shortly following. Among their many services, the shopping and delivery business has been a needed and wonderful addition that the customers truly love. The family run operation gives a personal touch while providing exceptional service.
