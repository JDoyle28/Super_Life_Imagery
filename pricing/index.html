Pricing

Our service provides you with shopped & delivered groceries / meals by Captains and Crews Boat Service to your requested destination.

Charges cover stocking perishable goods.

Shop and Drop
  • 10% of the gross grocery receipt total. (before savings)
  • Delivery Fee: $200 (Large orders requiring extra time, employees and runbacks are subjected to a double delivery fee)
  • Additional shopping/pickup location fees: $25
  • Porter fee: $10 (If applicable)
  • Optional stocking fee: Starting at $25 (Large orders and items requiring up stair put away may be subjected to a double stocking fee)
Create Your Menu
  • Create your menu fees start at $150
  • Fee covers 8, more than 8 based upon group and number of days
  • Plus Shop and Drop fee if applicable.
Catering
  • Pickup/Drop Off: Starting at $25 (For pickup/delivery orders with no shop n drop request $50)
