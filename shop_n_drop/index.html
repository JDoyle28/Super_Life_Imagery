Shop and Drop

Shop N Drop is an easy to use grocery shopping and delivery service. Simply fill out the shopping list and customer information form then send them to us. You will be contacted shortly to confirm your order and arrange for delivery. Your order will be filled fresh from local outlets and prompty delivered to your boat, home, or other location.

Add on Create Your Menu service to simplify your list creation.
Add on Catering for an easy trip starter.

How This Service Works:

  • Step 1: Fill out the order form options below and return them to us.
    • Order by Fax: print & fill out the forms, then fax them to 1-928-832-4250.
    • Order by Email: download & edit the forms, then email them to lakepowellgroceries@gmail.com.
  • Step 2: Shortly after we recieve your forms, you will be contacted to confirm your order and arrange for delivery.
  • Step 3: At the delivery time of your choosing, your items will be delivered. ** Stocking service is available.

To get started, download your forms.

Customer Information

Customer information form is needed to start your service and to reserve a delivery time.

Shopping List

Your shopping list may be submitted with the customer information or at a later date.

Billing Information

The billing information form is needed for credit card payment.

* Premium On-Site Catering service available. Meals prepared at your location. Contact us for full details of our highly reviewed service.

Unlike some other similar services that charge you when your order is placed, Lake Powell Groceries will not charge your credit card until your items are purchased, then promptly delivered. See pricing.
You have the option to fill out the customer information form only to reserve a delivery time and then send us your shopping list at a later date when you have made final choices. You also have the freedom to make changes to your order at any time prior to them being purchased.
